## 💡 MONETIZATION STRATEGIES

### How to Make Money:

1. **Transaction Fees** (Primary)
   - 1-2% per exchange
   - Lower fees for high volume
   - Free tier with limits

2. **Premium Subscriptions** (Secondary)
   - Pro: $9.99/month
   - Business: $49.99/month
   - Better rates + features

3. **Spread Markup** (Primary)
   - Buy at X, sell at X + 0.5%
   - Industry standard
   - Transparent pricing

4. **Withdrawal Fees** (Secondary)
   - $1-5 per withdrawal
   - Free for premium users
   - Covers payment processor costs

5. **Referral Commissions** (Tertiary)
   - Partner with payment providers
   - Earn commission on deposits
   - Passive income

6. **API Access** (Tertiary)
   - Charge developers for API
   - $99-499/month based on usage
   - B2B revenue
